# Privacy Policy

**At BitCSV Generator, your privacy is our top priority.**

We understand that you are using this tool to prepare highly sensitive data (passwords, usernames, website URLs). We have intentionally designed this tool with a strict privacy-first architecture.

### What data do we collect?
**None.** 

### 100% Client-Side
This application is a static webpage. There is no backend server, no database, and no data tracking mechanisms anywhere in the application.

- **No Analytics:** We do not embed any analytics scripts (like Google Analytics).
- **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do not share your activities with third-parties.
- **Local Browser Memory:** All CSV generation and data formatting happen locally within your web browser's memory. Once you refresh or close the page, the data is gone forever stringently.

### How your data is handled
When you create a credential, enter a website, or write notes, this text is read purely by the client-side JavaScript to generate a text blob formatted as a CSV.
When you download the CSV file, it is created securely from memory and saved directly to your local file system. The data is entirely isolated on your local machine.

### Your Responsibilities
Since this tool runs completely on your local device:
- You are responsible for the physical and digital security of the device running this tool.
- You are responsible for safely permanently deleting the generated `.csv` file from your device after importing its contents into Bitwarden.
